Different Breeds of Cats

There are many different breeds of cats, all with their own specific characteristics. Shown below is basic information about some of the most common cat breeds.

  • Ragdoll

    • Image by Amy Chen

    Ragdoll cats are known to be playful, intelligent, and affectionate. They can also be recognized by their fluffy, long-haired fur and their mellow disposition.

  • Maine Coon

    • Image by Mikhail Blinov

    The Maine Coon cat can weigh up to 20 pounds, and because of that and their friendly attitude, are known as gentle giants.

  • Persian

    • Image by Suramya Chandra

    Persian cats are widely considered to be the most beautiful breed of cat. Their fur requires daily brushing and occasional baths to prevent it from getting tangled.

  • British Shorthair

    • Image by John D

    The British Shorthair is one of the oldest English cat breeds, and are known for their hunting skill and strength.

  • Siberian

    • Image by Mona Magnussen

    Siberian cats are considered a "national treasure" in Russia, and have a dense triple coat and bushy tail. They are affectionate and playful.
